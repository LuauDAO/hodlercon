HodlerCon 2024 Locations

The location of HodlerCon 2024 will be determined by the price of ETH on June 15, 2023. Listed below are a few options for each price range. Beach Bum hodlers will use ranked choice voting to vote for one location in each price band until March 17th and the final location will be determined by which price band the ETH price falls in on June 15, 2023.
